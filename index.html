Our Events

Electrica

Held on 26th July, 2021

The first event of TechFest 2021, Event Electrica received 251 Registrations and competitors had to clear 3 elimination rounds.
Round 1: There was Technical quiz. And total there were 217 participants.
Round 2: There was a challenge to solve a Crossword Puzzle, Jumbled words and Word Search. From Second Round 102 students were qualified for Final Round.
Round 3: In this round Circuit Simulation skills of Students on Open Source Software were checked. Third Round was the final round and there were 6 participants.

Coding Warrior

Held on 27th July, 2021

This event provided students an opportunity to showcase their Coding Skills, Coding Warrior Event had only two coding rounds on HackerRank. The Event had 339 Registrations and in both rounds a problem statement was given and their perfect output was excepted. Submission time and Output was considered as selection criteria for next round and also the Winners.
Round 1: In First round there were 228 participants.
Round 2: In round 2 there were 72 participants and 3 were selected as winners.

Matholic Event

Held on 28th July, 2021

Matholic Event had 467 Registrations which was maximum in TechFest 2021. Entire event was further divided into 3 elimination rounds, where rounds 1 and 2 were quiz based on general questions from mathematics and final round was problem solving (either by solving manually or using open source platform). All the Three rounds were conducted with the help of Google Forms.
Round 1: In First round there were 385 participants and it was on Google Form.
Round 2: In second round there were 167 participants.
Round 3: This was the final round and there were 8 finalists out of which 3 were selected as winners.

Chemo Puzzle

Held on 29th July, 2021

Chemo Puzzle Event attracted 339 Students to Register and Compete together on basis of their knowledge and interest in Chemistry. This Event had 2 Rounds on Google Forms.
Round 1: First Round had Multiple Choice Questions, tested knowledge of Basic Chemistry. In First round there were 250 participants.
Round 2: Second Round was Solving a Crossword Puzzle. The Crossword had to be solved on paper and then submit via Google Form. In this round there were 119 participants and three were selected as a winner.

Issac Physics Event

Held on 30th July, 2021

Unlike other events, Issac Physics Event not only tested the knowledge of topic chosen by 72 registered students but also gave them an opportunity to showcase their skills to demonstrate and explain their choosen topic. The event consists of 2 Rounds.
Round 1: First Round was was uploading a video for explanation of topic. This round had 72 participants.
Round 2: In Round 2 Students had to explain their Choosen topic live to judges and answer all questions that were asked to them. The uniqueness of this event was that other students from PCCOE were also given chance to rate the videos they like via Feedback Portal. In round 2 there were 37 students selected as finalist out of which 3 teams (5 students) were winners.

Newtonian Mechanics

Held on 31st July, 2021

This was a unique event from the rest of the TechFest events, Newtonian Mechanics Event had special rounds. Total 35 students were registered.
Round 1: Students were asked to prepare an E-poster or Presentation on any Mechanics topic of their choice and upload it on drive. In First round there were 35 students.
Round 2: Selected students had to present and explain their topics infront of judges, also they had to answer all questions asked to them. In second round there were 21 selected students and out of which 3 teams (7 students) were selected as a winner.
